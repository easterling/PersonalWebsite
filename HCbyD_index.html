Welcome to Hall's Catering by Design

Serving Northwest Ohio and Southeast Michigan, Hall's Catering by Design (HCbyD) delivers a unique blend of flavors and catering services to fit any budget.

For over 30 years, executive culinary chef Reggie Hall, has been the caterer of choice for the Toledo Zoo, AP Federal Credit Union, Buckeye Cablesystem, Johnson Controls, the Toledo Refining Company, and non-profit events such as the "Toledo Zoo to Do" and "Relay For Life".
